Complaints

How to raise a concern about the UK Safety Camera Network, what happens next, and how to appeal.

Overview

Complaints reach USCN in one of two ways depending on what they are about. Operational complaints — how a camera is used, how data has been handled, day-to-day conduct of the network — are handled first by the Oversight Board. Complaints about a board or about the integrity of the network go directly to the independent Complaints Board and are not seen by anyone on the Oversight or Community boards.

Every complaint results in a written outcome sent to your email, detailing the board’s findings and any action taken.

Before making a formal complaint: if the issue is local — for example, a camera that appears to overlook your window — your Community Board neighbourhood representative, if one has been appointed for your area, is often the fastest first point of contact. You are also free to submit a formal complaint straight away, or to do both at the same time.

How an operational complaint is handled

  1. Your complaint is received and acknowledged by the Oversight Board.
  2. The Oversight Board investigates and reaches a provisional view. If it concludes that no further action is needed, that is the outcome and it is communicated to you in writing.
  3. If the Oversight Board concludes that action is required — for example, that a data request was mishandled, or that a camera needs to be repositioned, taken down, or its DPIA revised — the matter is referred to the independent Complaints Board.
  4. The Complaints Board reviews the matter and issues the final ruling, which is binding within the network. You receive the written outcome by email.

Complaints about a board

If your complaint is about the conduct of a board member, a board’s handling of a matter, or the integrity of the network itself, it goes directly to the independent Complaints Board. Complaints sent to this address are not shared with the Oversight Board or the Community Board.

The Complaints Board is structurally independent of the other boards and of UK Safety Camera Network Ltd, with strict eligibility rules covering board membership, employment, financial interest, and close family relationships. Its members declare and recuse themselves from any specific matter in which their independence could reasonably be questioned.

Appeals

Both an operational complaint outcome and a Complaints Board outcome can be appealed. Both routes are handled by the independent Complaints Board and use the same appeals mailbox — separated below only for clarity about which kind of decision you are appealing.

Appeal an operational complaint outcome

You have received an outcome from the Oversight Board or the Complaints Board on an operational complaint and want it reviewed.
Email
complaint.appeal@board.uscn.org.uk
Phone
0330 520 2254

Appeal a Complaints Board outcome

You have received an outcome from the Complaints Board on a complaint about a board and want it reviewed.
Email
complaint.appeal@board.uscn.org.uk

0330 520 2254
